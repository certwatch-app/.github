## üîç What is CertWatch?

CertWatch monitors your SSL/TLS certificates and alerts you before they expire ‚Äî via **Slack**, **Email**, **PagerDuty**, **Microsoft Teams**, or **Webhooks**.

- ‚úÖ **Certificate Expiration Monitoring** ‚Äî Get alerts at 30, 14, 7, and 1 day before expiry
- ‚úÖ **Chain Validation** ‚Äî Detect incomplete certificate chains that break API clients
- ‚úÖ **Weak Crypto Detection** ‚Äî Identify SHA-1, short RSA keys, and outdated TLS versions
- ‚úÖ **OCSP/CRL Revocation Checks** ‚Äî Know if your certificate has been revoked
- ‚úÖ **Team Collaboration** ‚Äî Role-based access for your entire organization
