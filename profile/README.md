# CertWatch

**SSL/TLS Certificate Monitoring Made Simple**

Never let an expired certificate take down your site again. CertWatch monitors your SSL/TLS certificates and alerts you before they expire.

## What We Do

- **Automated Monitoring** - Track certificates across all your domains
- **Smart Alerts** - Get notified via Email, Slack, or Webhooks at customizable intervals
- **Team Collaboration** - Manage certificates across your organization
- **Zero Configuration** - Just add your domains and we handle the rest
